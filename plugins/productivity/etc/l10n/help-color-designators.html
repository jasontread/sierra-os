MyProjects uses 3 color designators to identify late, upcoming and future projects and tasks.
  • Late items are those whose due dates are past the current date and identified using red highlighting or text
  • Upcoming items are those whose due dates reside between the current date and the user's upcoming date threshold (the default upcoming threshold is 14 days, this value is configured using the MyProjects - Preferences menu item) and identified using yellow highlighting or text
  • Future items are those whose due dates reside after the user's upcoming date threshold and identified using green highlighting or text
