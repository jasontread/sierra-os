As well as the ordinary ordered and unordered lists it is possible to use the styles %ROMAN%, %roman%, %ALPHA%, and %alpha%. The style is used in the first element of an ordered list, for example: 

# %ROMAN% Top level
## %ALPHA% Second level
## Second level
## Second level
### Second level
### Third level
## Second level
### Third level
#### %alpha% Fourth level
##### %roman% Fifth level
##### Fifth level
#### Fourth level
# Top level
# Top level

      2. Third level
    4. Second level
      1. Third level
        1. Fourth level
          1. Fifth level
          2. Fifth level
        2. Fourth level
  2. Top level
  3. Top level
